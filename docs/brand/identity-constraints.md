# IzignaMx Brand and Naming Constraints

Status: binding project rule
Last updated: 2026-07-20

## Canonical name

The brand name MUST always be written exactly as `IzignaMx`.

Disallowed variants include:

- Izigna
- IzignaMX
- IZIGNAMX in prose or UI copy
- Izignamx
- IZIGNA

Uppercase transformations are allowed only when they are a purely visual CSS treatment and the accessible text, metadata, source content, alt text, structured data, and copy preserve `IzignaMx`.

## Primary accent

The canonical IzignaMx accent is:

- IzignaMx Blue: `#3b82f6`

Orange MUST NOT be used as a brand accent, primary CTA color, focus color, active navigation color, project-wide highlight, or IzignaMx identifier.

## Supporting palette

- Deep Space: `#020617`
- Carbon: `#1b1b1d`
- Midnight: `#0b1120`
- IzignaMx Blue: `#3b82f6`
- Electric Cyan: `#22d3ee`
- Signal Teal: `#00b4c0`
- Stellar White: `#ffffff`
- Mist: `#f5f5f7`
- Lead Gray: `#66686a`

Additional spectral colors may be used only as contextual data visualization colors, simulated physical phenomena, project-specific identities, or status semantics. They MUST NOT displace IzignaMx Blue as the principal accent.

## Visual language

The project may draw from:

- Outer space and astronomical observation
- Celestial bodies and orbital systems
- Particle physics and field visualization
- Quantum-inspired probability and interference imagery
- Radio communications and telemetry
- Electromagnetic spectra and wave propagation
- Spacecraft, probes, observatories, and advanced engineering
- Futurism and restrained cyberpunk
- Solarpunk technology and regenerative systems

These references MUST remain coherent, legible, and functionally connected to the product narrative. They MUST NOT become arbitrary science-fiction decoration or pseudoscientific claims.

## Ethical constraints

- Do not use speciesist language or imagery.
- Do not promote animal exploitation.
- Prefer inclusive, accessible, and respectful language.
- Concepts involving food, commerce, or sustainability should favor plant-based and socially responsible scenarios when relevant.
